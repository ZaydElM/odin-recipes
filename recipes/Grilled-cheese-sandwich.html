grilled cheese sandwich

The image you see above is a photo of a grilled cheese sandwich, this is a recipe how to make it. In 8 easy steps you will be able to make it on your own in no time!

The ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1–2 slices of cheese
  • 1–2 tablespoons butter

The steps

Make sure you use the right amounts

  1. Spread butter on one side of each bread slice
  2. Place one slice in a pan, butter side down
  3. Add cheese on top
  4. Cover with the second slice of bread, butter side up
  5. Cook on medium heat until the bottom is golden
  6. Flip the sandwich
  7. Cook until the other side is golden and the cheese is melted
  8. Remove, slice in half, and serve
