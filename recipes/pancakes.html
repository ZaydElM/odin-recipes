Pancakes

The image you see above is a photo of pancakes, this is a recipe how to make it. In 10 easy steps you will be able to make it on your own in no time!

The ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1-2 tablespoons melted butter or oil

The steps

Make sure you use the right amounts

  1. Put flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt into a bowl
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together
  3. Add milk and the egg
  4. Stir until the batter is smooth
  5. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a little butter or oil
  6. Pour some batter into the pan
  7. Wait until bubbles form on the surface
  8. Flip the pancake
  9. Cook until both sides are golden
  10. Remove and serve
