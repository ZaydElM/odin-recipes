Fruit salad

The image you see above is a photo of a fruit salad, this is a recipe how to make it. In 7 easy steps you will be able to make it on your own in no time!

The ingredients

  • 1 apple
  • 1 banana
  • 1 orange
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1–2 tablespoons honey (optional)
  • A squeeze of lemon juice

The steps

Make sure you use the right amounts

  1. Wash all the fruit
  2. Peel the banana and orange
  3. Cut all the fruit into small pieces
  4. Put everything into a bowl
  5. Add honey and lemon juice
  6. Mix gently
  7. Serve fresh
